OOP Technology Roadmap

1. Basics

Learn the basics of Object-Oriented Programming including classes, objects, and methods.

2. Principles

Understand the core principles of OOP: encapsulation, inheritance, polymorphism, and abstraction.

3. Advanced Concepts

Explore advanced OOP concepts such as design patterns, SOLID principles, and refactoring.

4. Frameworks

Learn about popular OOP frameworks and libraries in different programming languages.

5. Projects

Apply your knowledge by working on real-world projects and contributing to open-source.
